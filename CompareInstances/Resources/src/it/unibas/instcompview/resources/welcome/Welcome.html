Welcome!

This application allows you to compare two instances choosing
between two different algorithms: Signature and Bruteforce.

You first have to load a Scenario.
You can load it by choosing its .properties file.

Once you have loaded it, you can double-click on it or press
the "Compare Instances" icon to run the algorithm.
It will display a new tab with all the information you need:
score of the applied algorithm, settings, tuple mappings and
value mappings.

To see what tuples have been left out,
because they couldn't be matched,explore the 2 databases.
By opening the tables, you will be able to see what tuples
have been excluded.
